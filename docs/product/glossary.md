# Mnelo Glossary

| Term | Definition |
| --- | --- |
| Project | A construction tender or active job workspace containing documents, BOQs, classifications, RFQs, quotes and procurement knowledge. |
| BOQ | Bill of Quantities. A structured or semi-structured list of work items, quantities, units and sometimes rates or amounts. |
| Entity | A structured object extracted from documents or BOQ rows, such as product, system, material, manufacturer, model, specification or quantity. |
| Knowledge | A reusable fact, classification, correction or decision that Mnelo can apply to future workflows. |
| Knowledge Graph | The connected model of projects, documents, BOQ items, entities, suppliers, quotes and decisions. |
| AI Agent | A specialized AI workflow that performs a task such as document classification, entity extraction, BOQ classification or quote analysis. |
| Capability | A durable product ability, such as BOQ parsing, RFQ generation or supplier matching. |
| Procurement | The process of sourcing, requesting, comparing, approving and buying materials, equipment or services. |
| Supplier | A company that can quote, provide or install a product, package or service. |
| Manufacturer | A company that makes a product or system component. |
| Distributor | A company that sells or distributes products from manufacturers to contractors or buyers. |
| Review Queue | A workspace for low-confidence or ambiguous AI outputs that require human review, correction or confirmation. |
| Confidence Score | A numeric measure of how certain Mnelo is about an AI, rules-based or learned decision. |
